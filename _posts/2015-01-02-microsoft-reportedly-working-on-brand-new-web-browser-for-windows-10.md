> Microsoft is said to be at work on an all new web browser, codenamed Spartan, that could officially move the company away from Internet Explorer upon the launch of Windows 10. ZDNet reports that the new app is not Internet Explorer 12, the next iteration of Microsoft&#8217;s longtime browser that we&#8217;d typically expect alongside another major Windows release.

While this is certainly not hot news I wanted to share it with you in case you missed it over the holidays. I think it&#8217;s nice to see Windows going forward with a new Browser, but I&#8217;m not sure how much it will actually change as long as a new IE version will still be shipped with a new Windows. Question is, which one will be the default browser. Anyway, hope to see a beta version of &#8220;Spartan&#8221; soon.
